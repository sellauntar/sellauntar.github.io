Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Echo. This privacy policy explains how we handle the information you send to us via email.

2. Information Collected

We collect only the email address of individuals who send us emails. This is the only personal data we collect and use.

3. Use of Information

The email address collected is used for two main purposes:

  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and communications related to our service.
  • Service Access: After purchasing access to our service, the email address will be used along with a password to authenticate and grant access to the service.

4. Protection of Information

We take reasonable measures to protect email addresses from unauthorized access. However, since email communication may not be entirely secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. User Rights

You may request the deletion of your email address from our records at any time by sending an email to echo-help@proton.me.

6. Cookies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies to collect additional information.

7. Changes to the Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at echo-help@proton.me.
